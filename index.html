A letter, written just for you
My Dearest,

I have been searching for the right words, the kind that capture something so full it overflows, and I keep finding that language alone is never quite enough.

But I want you to know: you are the most beautiful thing that has ever happened to me. Not beautiful like a painting you admire from a distance. Beautiful like a song that lives inside your chest, the kind you hum without even realizing it.

The way you laugh. The way you think. The way you look at the world with those eyes that somehow see everything more vividly than anyone else I've ever known. I find myself grateful, every single day, that I get to be near you.

✦   ✦   ✦

There are a thousand little things I love about you, the way you make ordinary moments feel like they matter, the warmth you carry without even knowing it, the way being with you feels like coming home.

I don't need the whole world. I just need you in it.

Always & entirely yours,
Yours ♡
P.S. — You probably didn't expect a love letter today. That's exactly why you deserved one.
